Typical Sequence of Documents & Approvals in a Development Project
Due-diligence reports (Phase I ESA, title, geotech, wetlands).
Zoning/land-use entitlements (rezoning, site plan, subdivision plat).
Environmental permits & studies (federal + state SEPA/CEQA + SWPPP).
Building-permit package (plans stamped by licensed professionals referencing the exact adopted code).
Construction-phase permits (grading, utilities, NPDES coverage).
Final inspections → Certificate of Occupancy + any bonds released.
